Hosted on the last day of 2020, amongst the hot sand dunes and gum trees of Little Desert National Park, on the dried salt lake of 'Eagle Swamp', the first edition of Trappefest welcomed an eclectic music, arts and lifestyle scene.
During a long hot summer day & night, we celebrated the end of a year and the beginning of a new one through games, workshops, live music and dj sets.
