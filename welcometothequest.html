“The spirit of the Trappe is in grave danger!
An unknown force is acting against the group and has broken up and scattered the pieces of a secret Trappe artifact that holds great power.
Without it the life force of the group could disappear forever!
You are therefore all called upon for a quest: Find the scattered pieces and put them back together, which will cast away the unknown force for good.
One of you, the one you call Thomas but that I shall refer to as ‘The witness’, has unwillingly become a part of it. He has seen the whole event and can therefore help you solve the various clues that have been left behind, but the evil force is stopping him from acting directly… You have the clues for the next step right here.”
